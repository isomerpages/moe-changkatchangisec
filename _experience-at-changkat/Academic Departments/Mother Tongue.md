<p><strong>Learning Outcomes:</strong>
</p>
<p>The Mother Tongue Department is committed to nurturing confident and culturally
rooted learners who can communicate proficiently in their Mother Tongue
languages in everyday life and in an increasingly interconnected world.</p>
<p>Beyond language proficiency, we believe that learning one's Mother Tongue
is a journey of self-discovery — one that deepens students' appreciation
of their ethnic heritage, values, and cultures. Through authentic and engaging
learning experiences, both in and beyond the classroom, students develop
not only linguistic skills but also the critical thinking, creativity,
and cross-cultural awareness that are essential for the 21<sup>st</sup> century.</p>
<p>Our programmes are designed to spark curiosity and a lifelong love for
the language, empowering students to be active, reflective learners who
can navigate a diverse and rapidly changing world with confidence and cultural
sensitivity.</p>
