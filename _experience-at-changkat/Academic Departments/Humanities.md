<p>At Changkat Changi Secondary School, the Humanities Department fosters
curiosity, critical thinking, and a deeper understanding of the world.
Through History, Geography, Social Studies and G1 Humanities, students
explore human experiences, and global challenges. We encourage the use
of inquiry and cultivate eCCE2021 communication skills, equipping students
with the knowledge and skills to engage with the world as informed and
empathetic citizens.</p>
<p>Our Secondary 2 students also go through a module which explores the interconnectedness
between History and Geography, called Interdisciplinary Humanities.</p>
<p>We aspire for our students to develop the ability to think independently
and make meaningful synergies across disciplines, and hope to nurture active
contributors to society.</p>

<p>The Humanities Department also offers a Talent Development Programme for
Secondary 2 G3 students who demonstrate a passion and aptitude for the
Humanities. These students go through a structured modular TDP programme
from Term 1-3, and are mentored by teachers who guide them on various Humanities
topics and train them for competitions. The close teacher-student mentorship
has enabled our students to win accolades at the following competitions:</p>
