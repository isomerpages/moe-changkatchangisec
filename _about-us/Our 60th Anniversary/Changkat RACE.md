<h2>CHANGKAT R.A.C.E. – 2026</h2>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Changkat 60/photo_1.png">
</div>
<p><strong>Rising.Above.Challenging.Experiences (R.A.C.E.)</strong> – serves
as both the tagline for this year's Changkat cross-country and our hope
for every student participant. To rise above means to be resilient. Life
will present us all with challenging experiences, but when we face these
moments with care for one another and respect for ourselves, we build the
strength to push through. This spirit of resilience, care and respect is
what drives Changkat's flagship cross-country event.</p>
<p>We invited Changkateers, alumni, parents and staff to run 3.6 km at the
Sports Hub vicinity, along the park connector by Geylang River. Our school
community turned out in great numbers for this 60<sup>th</sup> Anniversary
race!</p>

<p>The competition was fierce and spirited, with outstanding performances
from runners across all year levels. The Hawk House ultimately claimed
victory in the house standings, with Harrier, Eagle and Falcon houses also
showing strong performances.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Changkat 60/photo_4.png">
</div>
<p>A particularly heartwarming aspect of the event was our collaboration
with Sport Singapore, where we had the privilege of hosting a group of
seniors from the NTUC Health Active Ageing Centre. Our elected student
Community Ambassadors (CAs) demonstrated exceptional care and respect,
engaging warmly with the seniors through inclusive games, singing, and
joyful interaction. This inter-generational socialising not only enriched
the Changkat R.A.C.E. experience but also showcased our school's commitment
to caring for and treasuring all members of our community, embodying our
values of respect and care.</p>

<p>The Changkat R.A.C.E. was a memorable celebration of our school's 60<sup>th</sup> anniversary,
showcasing unity and active participation. The event highlighted our school’s
commitment to health, sportsmanship, and inter-generational connection.
As we continue our anniversary celebrations, we look forward to more opportunities
that strengthen bonds and create lasting memories.</p>
