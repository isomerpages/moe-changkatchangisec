<h2>60th Annual Speech &amp; Prize-Giving Day</h2>
<p><strong>Theme:</strong> Thrive Together, Inspire Excellence
<br><strong>Event Date:</strong> Friday, 10 April 2026, 3.00pm – 4.30pm</p>
<p>The 60th Annual Speech &amp; Prize-Giving Day is a meaningful occasion
for the school community to celebrate the achievements of our Changkateers
together with their families. It recognises the dedication and perseverance
of our students and staff, honours the accomplishments of our prize recipients,
and affirms the commitment of our student leaders as they step forward
to serve the school community.</p>
