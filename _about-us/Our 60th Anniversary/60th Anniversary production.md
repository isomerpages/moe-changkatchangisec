<h2>60th Anniversary Show @ OTH</h2>
<p><strong>Date:</strong> Friday, 5 June 2026, 5.30pm
<br><strong>Venue: </strong>Our Tampines Hub
<br>
<br>Changkat Changi Secondary School proudly presents a theatrical performance
that brings to life the rich tapestry of its journey through the decades.
Through a captivating blend of drama, music, dance and movement, students
from the Performing Arts CCAs will weave together a historic storyline
that reflects the school’s humble beginnings, its vibrant present and its
bold aspirations for the future. Each Act will echo the voices of generations
of Changkateers; highlighting moments of perseverance, growth and shared
pride that have shaped the school’s identity.
<br>As the story unfolds on stage, the performance will pay tribute not only
to the milestones of the school but also to the people who have been part
of its journey — students, educators, alumni and stakeholders. Their collective
spirit forms the heartbeat of Changkat Changi Secondary School - inspiring
today’s students to honour the past while stepping confidently toward tomorrow.
<br>With eyes set on the future, Changkat Changi Secondary School remains
committed to shaping resilient and compassionate individuals who are ready
to navigate the ever-changing landscape of the modern world and contribute
positively to society. Without further ado, put your hands together for
Dreams Sown, Destiny Awakened.</p>
