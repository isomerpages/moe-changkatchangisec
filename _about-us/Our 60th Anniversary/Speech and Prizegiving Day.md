<p><strong>Theme:</strong> Thrive Together, Inspire Excellence
<br><strong>Event Date:</strong> Friday, 10 April 2026, 3.00pm – 4.30pm</p>
<p>The 60th Annual Speech &amp; Prize-Giving Day is a meaningful occasion
for us to celebrate the achievements of our Changkateers together with
their families, while reflecting on the progress of the school over the
past year. It is an opportunity for the school community to recognise the
dedication and perseverance of our students and staff. Through this platform,
we honour the accomplishments of our prize recipients while also affirming
the commitment of our student leaders as they step forward to serve the
school community.</p>
<p>As the school celebrates its 60th anniversary this year, this special
occasion marks another milestone in recognising the achievements of our
Changkateers and the shared journey of our school community.</p>
