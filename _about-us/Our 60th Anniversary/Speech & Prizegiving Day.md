<h2>Speech &amp; Prize-Giving Day</h2>
<p>The Speech &amp; Prize-Giving Day is a meaningful occasion for the school
community to celebrate the achievements of our Changkateers together with
their families. It recognises the dedication and perseverance of our students
and staff, honours the accomplishments of our prize recipients, and affirms
the commitment of our student leaders as they step forward to serve the
school community.</p>
