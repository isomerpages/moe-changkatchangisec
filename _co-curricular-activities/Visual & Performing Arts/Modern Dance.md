<h3>CCA Objectives</h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To promote and inculcate dance appreciation</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To provide an opportunity for the participants to be exposed to different
genre of dance</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To establish team spirit &amp; dancers’ friendship bonds among dancers</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To build up leadership skills and grooming of leaders</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To provide a platform for internal and external performances</p>
</li>
</ul>
