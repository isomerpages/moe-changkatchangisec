<h3>CCA Objectives</h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Enhance understanding and appreciation of Malay culture and dance through
active participation in events and performances, fostering cultural awareness,
respect, and pride in the tradition.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop Malay dancers into confident, disciplined, and gracious youths,
nurturing self-discipline and instilling a deep sense of pride in their
craft.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Instill an appreciation for the diverse musical and rhythmic traditions
of Malay dance.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide opportunities for personal growth and character development through
Malay dance to cultivate resilience, focus, and perseverance in young dancers.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Promote inclusivity and diversity in Malay dance by embracing dancers
from various backgrounds and encouraging widespread appreciation of the
art form across communities.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>Key Programmes</h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Training of dance techniques</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Attend arts performances</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Participate in school events and external productions</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Engage in community events such as festive celebrations</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Camps and bonding games</p>
</li>
</ul>
