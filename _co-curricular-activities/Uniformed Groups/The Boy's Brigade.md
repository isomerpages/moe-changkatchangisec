<h3>CCA Objectives</h3>
<p>As the first Uniformed Group for Youths the Brigade grows Boys into leaders
of moral character who are loyal and responsible citizens. The Brigade
does this by promoting the habits of Obedience, Reverence, Discipline,
Self-Respect and all qualities that contribute to strong character and
responsible citizenship. The 40th Company executes this will by empowering
Boys to make decisions at all levels of their training; assigning boys
responsibilities based on their age and aptitude.</p>
<h3>Key Programmes</h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Core activities such as the Bagpipes and Drums band, adventure hikes and
overnight camps.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The development of Service Leadership through Community Service (BB Cares,
BB Share-A-Gift).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The development of the habits of discipline, self-respect, grooming and
teamwork through the structure of uniform, badges and drills.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Badgework to develop the habit of lifelong learning.</p>
</li>
</ul>
