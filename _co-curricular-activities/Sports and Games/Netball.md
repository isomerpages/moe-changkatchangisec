<h3>CCA Objectives</h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To develop character building, team spirit and sportsmanship through the
game</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To instill discipline and develop a sense of responsibility and resilience
through trainings and competitions</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>Key programmes</h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Recruitment of ‘C’ division players in January</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Weekly training twice a week on Tuesdays and Thursdays at the Parade Square
on skills and tactics of the game</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Preparation for and participation in the ‘B’ and ‘C’ Division East Zone
Inter-School Badminton Championships in January and February</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Preparation for and participation in the M1 School Challenge League from
March to May</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To organize friendly inter-school games at the end of the year</p>
</li>
</ul>
