<h3>CCA Objectives</h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To develop our students’ talents, skills and interests in Badminton</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To nurture our students to become athletes who exemplify the spirit of
sportsmanship</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To instill a sense of camaraderie among our students and build resilience
in our students</p>
<p></p>
</li>
</ul>

<h3>CCA Achievements</h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>2025 – ‘B’ Division Boys defeated Manjusri Secondary School and Bedok
Green Secondary School in the National School Games East Zone Badminton
'B' Division Championships</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>2024 – ‘C’ Division Girls defeated School of Science and Technology, Singapore,
Jurong West Secondary School and Guangyang Secondary School to emerge 2nd
runner up in the National School Games Badminton 'C' Division Championships
(League Round)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>2024 – ‘B’ Division Boys defeated Westwood Secondary School and Ang Mo
Kio Secondary School in the National School Games East Zone Badminton 'B'
Division Championships</p>
</li>
</ul>
